## What This Agent Covers

- Ruh-hosted cloud OpenClaw bundle for the ECC Estimating Agent.
- SharePoint and Company Cam auto-trigger support, manual chat/dashboard kickoff, and reviewer feedback events.
- Five sequential stages with independently rerunnable stages, audit logging, and revision diffs.
- Configurable ECC workbook pricing, rates, taxes, markup, mappings, thresholds, markets, trades, and notifications.
- Database result tables for projects, inputs, scope/evidence, estimates, QA/RFIs, artifacts, pricing config, connectors, audit logs, feedback, memories/rules, and diffs.
- No model training/fine-tuning; learning only through authorized memory/rule/config records.
- Approved custom skill bodies from skills/.

## What This Agent Does NOT Cover

- Ground-up construction estimating.
- Local, on-premises, customer-hosted, or desktop-only operation.
- Autonomous final estimate approval without Darrow/reviewer accountability.
- Phase 2 mobile/in-field co-pilot, LIDAR, Teams/Slack/SMS, PowerPoint proposals, or video proposals.
- Replacing SharePoint, Company Cam, ECC Excel pricing workbooks, NetSuite, BlueCollar, EagleView, or Google Maps systems of record.
